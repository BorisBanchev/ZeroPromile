# ZeroPromile User Manual

## Register

Fill your user details and create an account:

- Full name
- Email address
- Password (at least 8 characters long)
- Confirm Password
- Gender (Male or Female)
- Weight (Kg)

![Register Screen](../images/register.png)

## Login

Fill your user details and log in into your account:

- Email address
- Password (at least 8 characters long)

![Login Screen](../images/login.png)
